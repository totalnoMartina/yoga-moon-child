About my classes and offers...

I am a yoga instructor and massage therapist with a passion to travel around the world and to work with people through my skills and understanding of their needs. Influenced by my teachers, peers, environment and my travels I aim to bring unique sense of empowerment to each of my class. Through intention, meditation, pranayama and asana my goal is to have my students leave each class feeling refreshed and renewed. I also add some massage elements into my yoga classes to increase the best feelings for all my students. All my classes are delivered in English and Polish language and I put maximum hands to assist during the sessions.
